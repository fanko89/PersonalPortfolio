Design principles

design princinles are principles that are used to create visually appealing designs. This makes the design more functional for the end user. Below is some of my most used principles

Consistency

Is about how you need to make your designs simple and relevant and consistency. When designing, do not overcomplicate things and use too many different designs, instead make it memorable to your views. Make sure to have a logical design that isn't overpowering to the eye. This will confuse your audience and make it difficult to be memorable. Make sure to use appropriate design principles that go well togeather. simplicity is the key when it comes to colors and design, just keep that in mind on your next design.

Within the Lines

When designing you need to make sure you have things that are evenly space and line up in a sense. but be careful not to overdo it because if someone looks at your design and see everything the same size and shape you might get a little confused what you are looking at. You need to make sure to have your focused on the larger area than other parts that are not needing as much attention. This will have the view know what they are looking at.

Do not Interfere

When you are designing your website, you want to make it so there are no parts that might be distracting the user. This could lead to confusing. Make sure to keep with the things that people already familiar with. Do not go out of the norm or complex things where it should not need it. If you keep a nice clear path for the user to go and keep it clean and basic this will enhance your website traffic.

Simplicity Is The Key

When your designing something you need to keep simpicity in mind whe n making it. When adding a whole bunch of elements it can sometimes make the user confused on what is trying to be represented. The user might get overwhelmed and leave instead of engage in your design. Try asking your self what point am I trying to get across and how can I do it with the least amount of items.
