Why Is Home Safety Check-up so important?

Annually check-ups keep your family safe from dangers of electrical fires, damage or shock. Performing this valuable inspection reduces the potential failures in the future.

Why Hire A Pro?

Some electrical jobs seem doable to owners, but they don't realize how many electrical safety hazards there are when performing this job. ESFI website states, "Home electrical fires account for an estimated 51,000 fires each year, nearly than 500 deaths, more than 1,400 injuries, and $1.3 billion in property damage." This is an alarming fact that shouldn't be taken lightly. In the end, it's better to be safe then sorry, so message or call us today and keep your home or bussiness safe.
